## 📞 Key Decisions

### Decision 1: Scope
- **MVP**: Foundation + 1 pathway (6-8 weeks)
- **Full**: All 4 pathways (12-16 weeks)
- **Recommended**: MVP first, expand based on demand

### Decision 2: Accreditation
- **Open CPD**: Fast, cost-effective (start immediately)
- **CPDSO**: Max credibility (after 6 months)
- **Recommended**: Start Open CPD, add CPDSO in Y2

### Decision 3: Content
- **In-house**: Full control, slower
- **Vendor**: Microsoft/AWS content, faster
- **Recommended**: Hybrid (1/3 custom, 2/3 vendor)

### Decision 4: Community
- **Automated**: Forums with AI moderation
- **Managed**: Full-time community manager
- **Recommended**: Managed (worth the cost)
