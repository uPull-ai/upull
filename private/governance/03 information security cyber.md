# 3. Information Security and Cyber

*Part of the uPull.ai Public Sector Supplier Requirement Pack. See `00-overview.md` for the legal review note — this is not legal advice.*

NHS DSP Toolkit supplier guidance includes specific expectations around suppliers and contracts, and public buyers often require documented security controls proportionate to the service.

## Checklist

- [ ] Information security policy
- [ ] Access control policy
- [ ] Incident response plan
- [ ] Business continuity and disaster recovery plan
- [ ] Asset and device management policy
- [ ] Secure development and change management policy
- [ ] Vulnerability and patching procedure
- [ ] Evidence of Cyber Essentials or equivalent, where the opportunity requires it
- [ ] Subprocessor and hosting supplier list
