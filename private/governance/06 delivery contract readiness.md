# 6. Delivery and Contract Readiness

*Part of the uPull.ai Public Sector Supplier Requirement Pack. See `00-overview.md` for the legal review note — this is not legal advice.*

The Cabinet Office Model Services Contract is designed for complex, high-value public contracts and shows the level of structure public authorities may expect in services and IT contracting, even where a smaller supplier uses a simplified contract set.

## Checklist

- [ ] Master services agreement
- [ ] SaaS terms or software services schedule
- [ ] Order form template
- [ ] Statement of work template
- [ ] Service level schedule
- [ ] Support and maintenance policy
- [ ] Change control form
- [ ] Confidentiality agreement template
- [ ] Subcontracting approval process
- [ ] Exit and transition assistance outline
- [ ] AI transparency and human oversight statement
