# 5. Ethical and Social Compliance

*Part of the uPull.ai Public Sector Supplier Requirement Pack. See `00-overview.md` for the legal review note — this is not legal advice.*

The public procurement framework and wider policy environment increasingly assess ethical conduct, transparency, and responsible business behaviour as part of supplier due diligence and award readiness.

## Checklist

- [ ] Anti-bribery and corruption policy
- [ ] Modern slavery statement or policy
- [ ] Equality, diversity and inclusion policy
- [ ] Whistleblowing policy
- [ ] Fraud prevention policy
- [ ] Gifts and hospitality register
- [ ] Environmental and sustainability statement
- [ ] Carbon reduction plan, where required by the procurement
