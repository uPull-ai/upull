# 9. Draft Contract Set

*Part of the uPull.ai Public Sector Supplier Requirement Pack. See `00-overview.md` for the legal review note — this is not legal advice. These are drafting starting points, not finished contracts — each must be reviewed by a qualified UK solicitor before use in any live procurement or contract.*

Seven contract documents uPull.ai should hold, with purpose and the core clauses/fields each should include.

## Document 1: Mutual non-disclosure agreement
**Parties:** uPull.ai Ltd and the customer.
**Purpose:** To protect confidential information exchanged during discovery, pilots, procurement, and contracting.
**Core clauses:** definition of confidential information · permitted purpose · need-to-know access restrictions · security and care standard · return or deletion on request · exclusions for public information and independently developed material · standard carve-out for legal or regulatory disclosure · term and survival period.

## Document 2: Master services agreement
**Purpose:** Main contract for SaaS, advisory, implementation, and support services.
**Core clauses:** services and ordering mechanism · customer dependencies and cooperation · fees, invoicing, and payment terms · change control · service levels and support · data protection and security · confidentiality · intellectual property · warranties and disclaimers · limitation of liability · audit and records · FOIA cooperation wording for public customers · term, termination, suspension, and exit support · subcontracting controls · modern slavery, anti-bribery, equality, and compliance with law.

## Document 3: Order form
**Purpose:** Short deal-specific document linked to the master agreement.
**Fields:** customer name · contract start date · initial term and renewal · services/modules purchased · fees · implementation scope · support tier · named subprocessors or approved hosting environment · special terms.

## Document 4: Statement of work
**Purpose:** Defines professional services or onboarding projects.
**Sections:** scope and objectives · deliverables · milestones · customer dependencies · assumptions · acceptance criteria · project governance · project team · fees and expenses · change procedure.

## Document 5: Data processing agreement
**Purpose:** Governs processor obligations where uPull.ai processes personal data for the customer.
**Sections:** subject matter and duration · nature and purpose of processing · categories of data and data subjects · controller and processor obligations · security measures · subprocessor approvals · international transfers · audit and information rights · assistance with rights requests, DPIAs, and breach response · deletion or return at end of services.

## Document 6: Information security schedule
**Purpose:** Summarises the technical and organisational measures attached to the main agreement.
**Sections:** access control · encryption · logging and monitoring · incident response timings · backup and recovery objectives · secure development · vulnerability handling · personnel screening and training · supplier oversight · data segregation.

## Document 7: AI transparency schedule
**Purpose:** Explains where AI is used in the service and what safeguards apply.
**Sections:** AI-supported functions · human review and escalation points · accuracy and limitation statement · prohibited use cases · record-keeping and traceability · model and vendor dependency summary · data use boundaries · customer accountability for final decisions.
