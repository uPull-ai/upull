## Production Estimates

### Per Course (Typical)
- Instructional design: 40 hours
- Video production/scripting: 30 hours
- Interactive elements: 20 hours
- Assessment design: 10 hours
- Quality review: 10 hours
- **Total**: ~110 hours per course

### Total Project Effort
- 15 courses × 110 hours = 1,650 hours
- 4 FTE months for team of 3 designers/developers
- **Cost**: £40-60k for full course development

### Faster Alternative
- Use vendor content (Microsoft/AWS AI courses) - 50% faster
- Hybrid approach: 1/3 custom, 2/3 vendor - 30% faster

## Quality Assurance Checklist

Every course should have:
- [ ] Clear learning outcomes (SMART criteria)
- [ ] Tested assessments (80%+ pass rate on first attempt = too easy)
- [ ] Expert review (subject matter expert sign-off)
- [ ] Learner feedback (min 4.5/5 rating)
- [ ] Completion tracking (monitor dropout points)
- [ ] CPD verification (certificate can be verified)
- [ ] Accessibility review (WCAG 2.1 AA standard)
- [ ] Mobile optimization (works on phone/tablet)
